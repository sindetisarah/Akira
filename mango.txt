TERMINAL😎️-is an interface used to navigate through your computer ,folders and files rather than doing it manually.

The following are the commands i have learnt:
whoami 😊️ : shows you the user.
ls/dir 😊️ : shows the folders you have.
pwd  😊️   : shows the current folder.
cd      : opens a folder.
mkdir -p : creates the parent folder.
touch    : creates a file.
mv    😊️   :renames a file.
           for example;mv Frits.txt Vegetables.txt
cat  😊️    :opens a file.
           e.g cat fruits.txt
